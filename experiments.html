🔬 Physics Experiments

Simple, safe classroom-style demonstrations inspired by Julius Sumner Miller.

Try These at Home (With Adult Supervision)

🪙 Inertia Coin Drop

Place a card on a glass with a coin on top. Flick the card sideways and watch the coin drop into the glass.

💧 Surface Tension

Carefully place a paperclip on water. It can float due to surface tension even though metal is denser than water.

🌬 Bernoulli Ball Lift

Use a hair dryer and a ping-pong ball to show that fast-moving air can stabilize the ball.

🧲 Magnetic Induction

Move a magnet through a coil connected to a small meter to see current generated by motion.
